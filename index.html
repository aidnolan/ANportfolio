About Me

My name is Aidan Nolan and I'm a self-taught Web Developer based in London, England who is immediately available for permanent roles in Web Development.

I began studying coding intensively in early 2017 and it didn't take long to realise that this is where my passion lies. I have learnt a lot over the past two years and I'm eager to start in a professional role to show what I can contribute.

I have attended Meetups with the West London Coders group several times and have picked up invaluable tips from mixing with fellow coders and working together in groups to solve complex problems. My other interests include playing 7-a-side football, travelling the world, keeping up with current affairs, socialising with friends, playing video games and watching movies/TV.

I have used numerous online resources to facilitate my learning, some of which are detailed in the Courses Completed and In-Progress section below.
